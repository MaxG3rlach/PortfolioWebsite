About

I am Max Gerlach, an aviation photographer based in Breda, the Netherlands. Since I was young I have loved planes. Over the past years I have started photographing aircraft at my home airport, Breda International Airport (EHSE).

My main interest lies in historic aircraft — especially those of the Vliegend Museum Seppe based at EHSE. I have recently become a volunteer at the museum and serve as the Flight Coördinator.

My photos document warbirds, vintage machines and everyday airport operations. I shoot at air shows, fly-ins and museum events throughout the Netherlands.

All photos on this site are available for licensing. Reach me at mgerlach0168@gmail.com.
