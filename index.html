About me

Behind the Camera

Since I was young I have loved planes. Over the past years I've started photographing aircraft at my home airport, Breda International Airport (EHSE).

My main interest lies in historic aircraft — especially those of the Vliegend Museum Seppe based at EHSE. I've recently become a volunteer at the museum and serve as the Flight Coördinator.
