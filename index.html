The Photographer

Who I Am

Since I was young I loved planes. In recent years that turned into picking up a camera and heading out to my home airport — Breda International Airport (EHSE) — to capture the aircraft that live there.

My main interest is in historic aircraft, especially the ones from the Vliegend Museum Seppe based at EHSE. I've become a Volunteer at the museum and the Flight Coördinator — so I get pretty close to the machines I shoot.
